Features

in a single app

Replace phone calls and queues with full mobile control over your subscription, visits and payments.

Convenient booking

Pick a free time slot from the calendar and get instant confirmation. No phone calls required.

QR check-in

Roadside SOS

Multiple vehicles

How it works

Four steps and
you're subscribed

Download the app

Free on App Store and Google Play. Sign in with Apple ID or Google.

Choose a plan

From the basic seasonal tier to VIP with mobile service and priority SOS.

Book a slot

Pick a convenient slot in the station's calendar — confirmation arrives instantly.

Show your QR

Display the in-app code — the technician sees your vehicle and plan details right away.
